How It Works

Using StickyNotes is straightforward. Click 'Add A Note' to start, type your note, and it automatically saves. Edit by clicking on a note and pressing the edit button, and delete any note with a simple click on the delete icon. Your notes stay on your screen until you decide to remove them.
